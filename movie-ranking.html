Connor's best films

My top 5 favorite films in order

  1. Warrior (2011)
  2. Ford v Ferrari (2019)
  3. 9 (2009)

Warrior
2011

A stellar film with a great story, acting, and action. The fight sequences are among the most realistic. The relationships between the three leads drive the plot forward. A man's movie.

Ford V Ferrari
2019

The writing in this film is incredibly natural. No line or scene feels forced. The racing cinematography is thrilling and the score goes right along with it.

9
2009

The premise of the film is quite thought-provoking, and art style is very macabre and grim, which is cool. It's a very unique film.
